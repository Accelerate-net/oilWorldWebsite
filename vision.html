Vision, Mission & Core Values

Vision

Oil World OSC will focus on the customer’s business results, rather than our own, while ensuring to make the company a great place to work.

Mission

We will encourage our employees to creatively think in terms of desired outcomes, not just reactive, quick solutions and find ways to turn the ideal into reality. Customer service and lasting relationships as our number one priority, Oil World will strive to go beyond basic service expectations to help customers implement complete solutions.

Core Values

  • Optimism
  • Integrity
  • Loyalty
  • Wisdom
  • Organized
  • Reliable
  • Logical
  • Dedicated

Established in 2015, Oil World became a recognized name in the Oilfield industry when it comes to reputability, responsibility and service quality.
