Chemical Injection Skids

Oil World is specialized in the application, design and manufacture of transportable, permanent and skid-mounted chemical injection systems. These systems can be of standard design or built to customer bespoke requirements to deliver the full range of production chemicals. Our chemical injection pump units range from a single pump application to multiple pneumatic injection pumps with discharge flow rates of 0 to 2500 litres/hour and pressures from 0 to 13,000 psi.

The pumps are constructed from stainless steel which can be trimmed for various applications. We also design Containerised flow improver systems for extreme environmental conditions. These units are fully insulated and contain chemical storage tanks allowing the units to function for longer periods of time without the need for logistical support.

We offer:

  • Single-Point Chemical Injection Systems
  • Multi-Point Chemical Injection Systems
  • Gas-Powered Chemical Injection Systems
  • Chemical Injection Systems for Extreme Environmental Applications
  • Chemical Pump Skids/Containerised Pump Skids
  • Barrel Handling
  • Fuel Transfer Pumps
  • Equipment Hire

Tanks and Chemical Services

Oil World has a full range of DNV 2.7-1 offshore chemical and acid tanks. Tanks are used for the safe handling, transportation and storage of chemicals in oil and gas including waste liquids/oils, potable water and helifuel both on and offshore. Our tanks are designed and manufactured to international standards to withstand challenging offshore environments.

The national and international regulations regarding the filling, holding, storage, transportation and disposal of chemicals and acids are a challenge and we have the requisite knowledge to handle and manage your chemical requirements. Our tanks conform to industry standards and are inspected in accordance with DNV 2.7-1/EN 12079.

The fleet includes:

  • Acid Tanks
  • Heli fuel Tanks
  • Chemical Tanks
  • ISO Tanks and spreader beam if required

Product Overview

  • Designed, manufactured and certified to DNV 2.7-1/EN 12079 standards
  • Vessel material – 316 Stainless Steel In addition, we can provide:
  • Crossover supply for the bottom outlet connection
  • Spreader beams
  • Sling assemblies

Emergency Shutdown Systems

Oil World manufacture and supply various types of Emergency Shutdown Devices (ESDs) designed for Well Testing Applications. As a company standard our systems are all fully manufactured from SS316 stainless steel for use in harsh working environments.

System Features:

  • Fully enclosed option is IP 65 compliant
  • Equipped with pneumatic/gas driven pumps
  • Standby hand pumps in case of emergency when pneumatic supply is not available
  • Equipped with air regulators and filters for smooth operation
  • Colour engraved front panel showing user friendly flow scheme for ease in operation
  • Inbuilt tank of customised holding capacity based on application including tank instrumentation such as level switch, level indicator etc.
  • Equipped with inbuilt pneumatic/low pressure hydraulic logic for local and remote emergency shutdown
  • Provision for hook up and safety interlock with fusible plugs and flow line pilots
  • Interlock facility with various process equipment

Hydraulic Power Units

Oil World can design and manufacture an extensive range of Hydraulic Power Units (HPUs) to meet exact client requirements and specifications. Our hydraulic power units are designed to provide a safe and reliable hydraulic supply to a wide range of end-user equipment operating in hazardous environments.
