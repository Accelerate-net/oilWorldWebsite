Oil world is a stocking distributor of Pipes, Pipes Fittings and Valves used in Oilfield industry. Our ability to customize fittings and valves helps to meet immediate requirements where a made-to-order is not practical due to time constraints. The stocks are regularly updated and inspected to ensure the quality and quantity which our clients expect.

  • All range of Line Pipes (SMLS, SAW, ERW)
  • Fittings and Flanges
  • Piping Accessories and commodities
  • Hot Tap Tees, Barred Tees and Elbows
  • Relief Valves & ESD Systems
  • Manual Valves

Established in 2015, Oil World became a recognized name in the Oilfield industry when it comes to reputability, responsibility and service quality.
