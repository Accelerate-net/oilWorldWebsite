Industrial Maintainance

Oil World offers a comprehensive range of maintenance and repair services. Our access personnel are trained to internationally recognized standards and have a broad range of trade qualifications and experience in all aspects of offshore and onshore facility repairs. Oil World is a specialist in cold repairs to castings, on-site machining, welding, precision machining in a workshop environment, alignment (laser and optical) and pump refurbishment. With customized project tracking, extensive quality management policies & procedures, and the availability of on-site project management and administration teams, we stand different from our competitors in the industry.

Our Maintenance services includes:

  • Welding, weld repairs and Structural Steel works
  • Pumps, Motors, Heat Exchangers – Repair, Overhaul & Maintenance
  • Concrete works including heavy duty foundations
  • Industrial Electrical Installations
  • Plant-shutdown maintenance planning and management
  • Crane structure and hydraulics repairs and replacement
  • Pressure vessel welding and repairs
  • Pipe cutting and fitting

Flow Measurement and Control

The Oil and Gas industry is one of those areas that rely on flow measurement and control products to ensure efficiency, safety and accuracy in production. Starting from simple flow meters to complicated automated valve packages, Oil World handles a variety of products and services to analyze and improve the existing plant capacities. Our long-term relationship with manufacturers and well-trained engineers offers quick solutions to your flow control requirements with comprehensive installation and commissioning support.

  • Flow Meters (Magnetic, Vortex, DP, Positive Displacement, Open Channel etc.)
  • Turbine Flow Meters and Auxiliary Instruments
  • Control Valves, Shutdown Valves, Motor Operated Valves
  • Pressure Relief Valves, Pressure Safety Valves, NRVs
  • Regulators, Controllers and Governors
  • Orifice Plates
