About Us

Who We Are?

Established in 2014, Oil World became a recognized name in the Oilfield industry when it comes to reputability, responsibility and service quality.

What We Do?

We provide easy and reliable solutions to our international and domestic customers by supplying high quality materials, maintenance services and manpower solutions. Our services meet critical industry requirements, and we offer technical support to manage project activities which makes us your right partner.

How are We Different?

Being a trusted solution provider, it is our motto to serve all customers equally and efficiently. Our key strengths are,

  • Collaboration - Working in close partnership with our customers ensure that we understand their needs and meet/exceed their expectation.
  • Innovation - Technical capabilities and creativity know-how to make great ideas work. We develop innovative solutions that add value and speed to our services and supplies.
  • People - Experts, experienced and eager to collaborate. We are determined to deliver the best - every time.
  • Global Capability - Our unrivalled global reach, manufacturing footprint and integrated planning process, to deliver solutions which add real value, with consistent quality, across the world.

Our Services

Industrial Maintainance

Oil World offers a comprehensive range of maintenance and repair services.

Pipes, Valves And Fittings

Oil world is a stocking distributor of Pipes, Pipes Fittings and Valves used in Oilfield industry.

Instrumentation

Oil World’s professional team can assist in design, supply, service and troubleshoot instrumentation systems typically used by Oilfield operators.

Electrical

Oil World offers electrical services for a diverse range of Oil and Gas and Power industries.

Flow Measurement And Control

The Oil and Gas industry is one of those areas that rely on flow measurement and control products to ensure efficiency, safety and accuracy in production.

OCTG & Accessories

Our team is proficient in the supply of Oil Country Tubular Goods (OCTG) to middle east in various diameters, grades and wall thicknesses to meet the specific requirements of each wells.

Downhole Tools And Equipments

Oil World extends its support to drilling by offering solutions for daily challenges in rig operations.

Manpower

We provide premium quality labour supply services to all middle east countries with special focus on Iraq, Libya, South Sudan and UAE.
