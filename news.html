COVID-19: Oil World resumes site activities in full schedule

It has been a few months since the outbreak sent shockwaves through the global economy. Although the crude oil prices improved to support the industry and projects on hold are rescheduling to complete, many organizations continue to face deep challenges. It is important for everyone to update their strategies to live with the virus!

Iraq’s oil industry is slowly recovering from the Covid19 crisis and foreign oil companies have scheduled to send their team back to site. Oil World restarted the site activities in Kurdistan region in association with MNR and field operators, strictly complying to all local and international Covid19 protocols & regulations. We are on schedule to complete the line pipe B1-B2 project in Sarsang block as planned.

Although it is still unknown how things will change in coming months, we are hoping that the changes will be in everyone’s favour.

Stay safe and healthy for a better tomorrow!
